# Dashboard

The dashboard at **proxyhive.org** is your account home. From here you manage your
appliances, boards, endpoints and rewards.

## Overview

- **Appliances** — set up and manage your boxes. See [Installation](/getting_started/installation).
- **[Devices](/features/webinterface/devices)** — every board across your
  appliances, with health, proxy status and the box it belongs to.
- **[Endpoints](/features/webinterface/endpoints)** — your residential proxy slots.
- **[Rewards](/features/webinterface/rewards)** — your UNetwork earnings.
- **[Settings](/features/webinterface/settings)** — account settings.
