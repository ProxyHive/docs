Title: Devices
---
The **Devices** page lists every board across all your appliances.

## Columns

- **Label** — the board's name.
- **Health** — connection lamps for the board (presence, proxy, ADB).
- **App** — for appliance boards this shows the **box (appliance) name** the board
  hangs off.
- **Proxy** — the board's residential exit is live.
- **Device code** — the board's unique code.

![The Devices page listing appliance boards with health, the owning box name in the App column, license and device code.](/images/features/devices.png)

## Actions

From a board you can view its live screenshot, rotate its exit IP, and open its
details. Board management (mirror, license) happens in the
[Farmer app](/features/ADB).
