# Rewards

The **Rewards** page shows your UNetwork earnings across your boards.

- Rewards accrue as your boards build **uptime** on UNetwork behind their assigned
  endpoints.
- Only genuine UNetwork traffic earns — endpoints used for anything else are
  detected and penalised. See [Traffic Usage](/rules/traffic_usage).

> 📷 **Screenshot:** _The Rewards page showing UNetwork earnings over time._

New to UNetwork? Follow [Getting Started](/getting_started/requirements) to set up
an appliance and migrate your boards.
