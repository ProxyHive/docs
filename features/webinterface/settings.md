# Settings

The account **Settings** page manages your ProxyHive profile and preferences.

- Account details and login.
- Notification preferences.
- Security settings (including two-factor authentication, used when logging in to
  the Farmer app).

> 📷 **Screenshot:** _The account Settings page._

> Note: appliance hardware settings — USB/OTG slots and fan control — live on the
> **appliance's own** Settings page, not here. See [Box Control](/features/box_control).
