# Fair Use

ProxyHive is intended **solely** for running **UNetwork** on a headless board or an
unused physical phone. Following these rules is not hard — but they are strict, and
violations are detected.

## Not allowed

- **No private use of any kind.** ProxyHive is **not** for your private phone.
- **No phones with SIM cards.**
- **No browsing, video, images, games or downloads** over an endpoint. Even
  downloading a meme from tenor.com or browsing a Facebook page while the proxy is
  enabled is a violation.
- **No hotspots** on the boards.
- **No sharing** of endpoint network traffic with other devices.

## Consequences

All violations lead to a **permanent ban** from using ProxyHive, **without any
refund** of your endpoints.

## Clean your boards

If a board still has apps like Facebook, TikTok or Instagram installed, **delete
them**. The appliance [Debloat](/features/debloat) function helps, but you are
responsible for limiting a board's traffic to **UNetwork only**.

See [Traffic Usage](/rules/traffic_usage) for the specific traffic that is detected
and banned.
